Self-Defense Tips

1. Stay Aware of Your Surroundings

Always be conscious of your environment, especially in unfamiliar places. Avoid distractions like mobile phones while walking alone.

2. Trust Your Instincts

If a situation feels unsafe, trust your gut and remove yourself immediately.

3. Use Your Voice

If you sense danger, use a strong and firm voice to attract attention and deter attackers.

4. Learn Basic Self-Defense Moves

Take self-defense training to learn simple but effective moves to protect yourself.

5. Carry Safety Tools

Keep personal safety devices like pepper spray, a whistle, or a stun gun.

6. Stay in Well-Lit Areas

Avoid walking alone at night in dark or isolated places. Stick to well-lit paths.

7. Learn to Use Common Objects as Weapons

Keys, pens, or even a handbag can be used to defend yourself if necessary.
